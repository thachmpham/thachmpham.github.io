## Speech Analysis
### Practice: Mind Map

- Download transcripts.
- Draw mind maps.

### Practice: Transcript Summary

- Download transcripts.
- Two-column table.
- Transcript in column 1.
- One-line summary per section in column 2.

### Practice: Speech Summary

- Listen to a speech.
- How many points.
- Note one word per point.
- Read transcript.
- Highlight sentences sum up the whole section.
